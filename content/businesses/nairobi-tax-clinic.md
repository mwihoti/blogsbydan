# Nairobi Tax Clinic

## Daily Activities
The team helps Kenyan SMEs clean up receipts, separate personal and business spending, prepare tax reminders, and understand what their numbers mean before problems become expensive.

## What They Deal With
Founders often mix M-Pesa, bank, and cash records. Many only look at taxes when deadlines arrive.

## Content They Want
Short, practical explainers that turn confusing tax or compliance news into immediate founder actions.
