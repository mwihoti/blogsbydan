Nairobi Tax Clinic speaks to founders like an operator, not like a corporate accountant. The founder believes most SMEs do not have a tax problem first; they have a records problem. Good content should tell founders exactly what to check today: sales records, receipts, payroll, VAT, supplier payments, and personal spending mixed with business accounts.

The main offer is a Ksh 2,000 tax health check. It gives the founder a simple list of what is missing, what is risky, and what to fix before the next filing deadline.
