Team1 Kenya Mini Hack is a 3-month builder accelerator for Kenyan engineers, founders, and teams who want to ship real products on Avalanche. The programme is practical and execution-focused. It is not just a theory class or one-day hackathon. Builders complete quests, attend sessions, pick real bounty problems, build working MVPs, and prepare for judging. The audience includes Kenyan developers, founders, students, and startup teams who want to learn Avalanche by building useful products. The tone should be direct, builder-focused, urgent, and practical. Emphasize shipping, real products, Avalanche infrastructure, MVPs, community, and opportunities like bounties, grants, and visibility.
